Life Insurance

Life insurance is a contract between the policyholder and the insurer, where the insurer provides financial compensation to the policyholder’s beneficiaries in the event of the policyholder’s death. It ensures financial security for loved ones by offering a death benefit or payout.

Why Life Insurance

    Provides financial support for dependents after the policyholder's death.
  • Debt Coverage: Pays off outstanding debts (e.g., mortgage, loans) so that the family doesn’t bear the burden.
  • Estate Planning: Helps in wealth transfer and settling estate taxes.
  • Peace of Mind: Assurance that loved ones will have financial security in uncertain situations.
