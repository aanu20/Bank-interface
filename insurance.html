Life insurance

A contract between an insurance company and a policyholder, where the insurer pays a sum of money to the beneficiary when the policyholder dies.

Motor insurance

Protects vehicles against theft, accidents, and damage.

Home insurance

A general insurance policy that protects the structure and contents of your home from natural and man-made disasters

Health insurance

A contract between an insurer and an individual or group, where the insurer agrees to cover medical expenses.

Life Time insurance

A type of life insurance policy that covers the policyholder for their entire life.

Travel insurance

Provides financial protection for you and your loved ones while you are traveling
